what is this?

This page offers a simple form that automates bitly short link creation with your own bitly account. With this, you can create campaign names, and optionally specify team member names/initials. With this information the form will construct unique campaign URLs in the form "campaign_initials" and then return you the bitly short links. You can then log into bitly and view the statistics to easily track which links got the most traction on your team. This will help you target and optimize your efforts. This form can be run locally or on a webserver.
